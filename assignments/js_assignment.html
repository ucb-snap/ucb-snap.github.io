Change colors of Snap

Hi everyone. Today assignment is changing colors of Snap.
Follow these steps

1) Go to https://github.com/arctan5x/Snap-Decal-Fall15.

2) Download the zip file and unzip it in your local machine.

3) open index.html with a browser. You should see Snap! language rendered.

4) Change colors of multiple elements in Snap. You have browse through JavaScript files to see where you can change colors.

5) Take a screenshot of your changed Snap! and send it to tony.lee@berkeley.edu.
